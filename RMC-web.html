About Us

Rose Mary College of Arts and Science for women is run by Rose Mary Educational Society. This College is a natural outcome of the growth of the Matricultion Hr.Sec.Schools, Model School and CBSE Schools which have been doing creditable service to students since 1976. The College aims to give women of this locality a chance to get good and modern education at a reasonable cost.
